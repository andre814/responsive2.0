Hello There!

I'm Laura! I'm a UX/UI/Web designer living in Minneapolis, Minnesota. I'm graduating from the University of Minnesota in Spring 2018 (low-key dying) with a B.F.A. in Graphic Design and a minor in Computer Science.

Working with the public for the past 5 years has taught me a lot. There are things that everyone learns like what to do when a customer is screaming at you or general organizational skills, but the lessons that have stuck with me the most are that of empathy for the user. Between my various jobs at the U of M, I have seen everyone from children to faculty with multiple PhD's struggle with technology. It's an eye opener.

Because of these experiences, I design with the user's needs always on my mind. The goal is to be an advocate for the user, just in case no one else is.

Take a peek at my resume and we can get something started!
